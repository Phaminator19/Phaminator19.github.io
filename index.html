Laser Target-Shooting

Group 6 UCF Senior Design - Fall 2022 Spring 2023

Project Description Watch Our Showcase Video

Project Description

Our project aims to bring the firing range experience to your home by creating a laser rifle and an electronic target board. The Target Board will light up whenever the Laser Rifle is used to shoot at the target. The Target and the Rifle system will be connected using a smartphone app to play a variety of games and configuring their settings.

Motivation

  • - Our project is intended to be a way to entertain yourself as well as function as an educational firearm safety training tool for people who are concerned about handling live firearms.

  • - Aim to be fully functioning in any lighting environment (day or night) and involves the development of a mountable rifle scope compatible with our custom night vision vision system.

  • - Aim for customizability by using the smartphone app to change features such as the rifle's rate of fire and gunfire sound.

Goals

  • - Create a laser-target shooting system that gives immediate feedback to where a shot is made via LEDs.

  • - Portable system usable in both broad daylight and nighttime.

  • - Develop a smartphone app to control system for customizability.
